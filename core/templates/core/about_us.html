About Us

Read About Project Impact

Project Impact is an app designed to provide young adults aging out of the foster care system with an online platform to connect with other adults who can empower them through mentorship. The idea for Project Impact came about from a group of individuals who had a desire to impact a group who at times do not have all the resources available to them to navigate through finding a job, applying to college, and much more. Through mentorship, young adults aging out of the foster care system can learn how to thrive as they transition into the responsibilities of adulthood. Mentorship provides a way to encourage, empower, and equip foster care children to know they are valuable and capable of succeeding. On this app, people will have the opportunity to learn how to give back to the foster care community, find resources to learn more about helping young adults aging out of the foster care system, and sign up to mentor future leaders. We hope you find a way to participate in Project Impact!
